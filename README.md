# CrowdShield AI

## Intelligent Crowd Management & Predictive Risk Analysis Platform

CrowdShield AI is an AI-powered crowd management system designed to enhance public safety in high-density environments such as railway stations, stadiums, festivals, airports, metro stations, smart cities, and large public gatherings.

The platform combines Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Predictive Analytics, and Computer Vision to monitor crowd behavior, detect potential risks, and provide dynamic evacuation guidance before incidents occur.

---

## Problem Statement

Large gatherings often face challenges such as:

* Overcrowding
* Stampedes
* Congestion
* Delayed emergency response
* Lack of real-time crowd visibility

Traditional monitoring systems are reactive and depend heavily on manual observation.

CrowdShield AI provides a proactive and intelligent approach to crowd safety.
